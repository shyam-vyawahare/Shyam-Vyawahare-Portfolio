2026 HTML • CSS • JavaScript • Flask • Bootstrap • Responsive Design
View project on GitHub

Project Overview

Women Rights Compass App is a socially impactful platform designed to spread awareness about women's rights, safety resources, and legal support systems through an accessible digital interface. The application aims to empower women by providing information related to legal rights, emergency assistance, safety measures, and support services in a user-friendly format. The platform focuses on improving accessibility to important resources while promoting awareness, safety, and empowerment through technology-driven solutions.

Key Features

  • Women rights and legal awareness information system
  • Emergency contact and support access module
  • User-friendly and responsive interface
  • Safety guidance and resource navigation
  • Awareness-focused content organization
  • Quick access to important helpline information
  • Mobile-friendly and accessible design
  • Structured information delivery for ease of use

The Women Rights Compass App promotes awareness, empowerment, and digital accessibility to important women safety and legal resources. The platform demonstrates the use of technology for social impact by simplifying access to rights-related information and support systems.
